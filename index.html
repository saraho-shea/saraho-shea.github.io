Web Dev & Design

Hello, my name is Sarah O'Shea and I am a web developer based in NYC.

I am a Manhattan transplant originally from Fort Lauderdale, Florida. I first got my start in the tech world at a small mobile app startup. I instantly fell in love with the startup environment and the waves that you are able to make in a technical space. Although I am equipeed with a BS in Information Technology I decided to further my web development knowledge by attending Dev Bootcamp in NYC. At Dev Bootcamp I was trained in full stack with an emphasis on Ruby on Rails and Javascript. We learned using a number of different metholodgies all while practicing an agile workflow and test driven development. During my final projects at Dev Bootcamp I discovered my real passion for front-end web development, often taking the front-end lead for the project I was working on. I think a good user experience can make such a huge difference in the delivery of a final product. It is a passion of mine to be able to hear a client's vision for a product and apply it in a technical sense. I currently have a few front-end projects in the works so stay tuned for updated in the featured works section. When I'm not developing for the web I'm probably exploring new restaurants or coffee shops, sharpening my yoga skills, or planning my next trip.
